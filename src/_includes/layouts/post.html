Archives Exhibition “Gunung Api Jassin, Lahar Panas Chairil: Dari Dokumentasi H.B. Jassin”

Published by admin on July 15, 2022 July 15, 2022

“Gunung Api Jassin, Lahar Panas Chairil” (Jassin Volcano and Chailir Hot Lava) is a literature exhibition raised about Chairil Anwar and H.B. Jassin sourced from the collections of H.B. Jassin Literature Documentation Center. The exhibition will be curated by Hasan Aspahani and Esha Tegar Putra and will be held from July 11 to August 11, 2022, placed in H.B. Jassin LDC Gallery, Ismail Marzuki Park.

courtesy: Dispusip DKI Jakarta
